The ADEPT Product Loop

A method built for
intelligent systems.

Our signature process grounds every sprint in proven design and delivery frameworks — Double Diamond, Google Design Sprint, and Scrum — adapted for AI + IoT product development.

A
Align

Clarify the user, system boundaries, success metrics, and constraints — power budget, connectivity, cost, deployment environment. No assumptions carried forward.

D
Design

Rapid solution exploration using sprint logic: understand, sketch, decide, prototype, validate. The fastest path to a decision is a tested concept, not a long document.

E
Engineer

Build the device, firmware, data pipeline, and edge AI model in iterative delivery cycles. Security is integrated throughout — not added at the end.

P
Pilot

Deploy a controlled fleet. Measure uptime, data quality, model behaviour, and security posture. Treat the pilot as a rigorous experiment with defined success criteria.

T
Transfer

Hand over toolkits, documentation, runbooks, and governance artefacts. Your team inherits the capability to operate, update, and iterate — independently.
