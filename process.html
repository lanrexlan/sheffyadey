The ADEPT Product Loop

A structured operating method for AI + IoT delivery.

We run engagements as disciplined, evidence-driven loops that move from alignment to transfer with clear outputs at each step.

A
Align

Define user outcomes, deployment realities, integration points, and success criteria.

D
Design

Prototype architecture choices and de-risk high-impact decisions early.

E
Engineer

Build firmware, cloud, data pipelines, and model behavior as one system.

P
Pilot

Deploy controlled fleets, observe real performance, and tune from field evidence.

T
Transfer

Deliver handover assets, training, and governance artefacts to client teams.

Timeline and collaboration

Typical engagements run 4–6 weeks. Weekly demos, shared sprint boards, and operational checkpoints keep stakeholders aligned without slowing delivery.

Trust and delivery principles

  • Security-by-design and responsible AI controls.
  • Transparent progress with measurable sprint outputs.
  • Documentation and runbooks treated as first-class deliverables.
