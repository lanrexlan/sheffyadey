FAQ

Answers to common product and delivery questions.

These are the questions teams usually ask before starting an AI + IoT engagement.

Scope and fit

What kinds of products do you build?

Connected products where sensing, embedded logic, and AI inference must work together—industrial monitoring, smart infrastructure, logistics intelligence, and compliance-critical systems.

Who do you work with?

Internal innovation teams, product leads, and operations groups that need execution capacity and deep technical guidance.

Timelines and delivery

How long does a project take?

Discovery and architecture can be 1–2 weeks. Pilot-ready build sprints typically run 4–6 weeks, depending on complexity and hardware lead times.

Do you do pilots and prototypes?

Yes, but every pilot is designed as a path to productisation with observability, security controls, and transfer materials included.

Can you support full productisation?

Yes. We can continue from pilot through staged rollout, including fleet operations baselines and internal team enablement.

AI + IoT specifics

How much AI can run on-device?

It depends on model size, memory budget, latency requirements, and power profile. We benchmark these tradeoffs early and choose practical deployment targets.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes. We offer structured support windows covering model drift checks, incident response guidance, and controlled release updates.

How do we get started?

Book a discovery call with your constraints, business objective, and deployment context. We will propose the shortest viable delivery path.

Still have questions?

Bring them to a discovery call and we will work through the technical and delivery implications together.
