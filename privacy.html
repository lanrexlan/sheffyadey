Plain-language summary

This page explains what information may be collected when you contact Sheffy Adey, how that information may be used, and what choices you have. It is written for clarity and should be reviewed by the business owner or legal adviser before final production use.

1. Information we may collect

  • Contact form details: name, email, organization, and project message.
  • Communication records such as email threads or call notes.
  • Website usage data if analytics is installed.
  • Technical metadata such as browser type, device type, and approximate IP-derived location where analytics tools collect it.
  • Files or project materials voluntarily submitted by visitors.

2. How we use information

  • Respond to enquiries.
  • Scope possible projects.
  • Prepare proposals or discovery calls.
  • Improve website and services.
  • Maintain basic communication and project records.
  • Protect the website from misuse or suspicious traffic.

3. What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal information.
  • We do not request sensitive personal information through this website.
  • We do not use contact messages for unrelated marketing without permission.
  • We do not knowingly collect children’s data through the website.
