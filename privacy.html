Privacy

Practical privacy information for website visitors.

This page explains what information the Sheffy Adey website may collect, how we use it, and how to contact us with privacy questions.

Plain-language summary

We aim to use only the information needed to operate this website, respond to enquiries, and improve visitor experience. This page may be updated as our website tools, hosting setup, and communication channels evolve.

Information we may collect

Where relevant, we may collect technical usage data (such as browser type, device class, pages visited, and referral source), plus contact details and project context you choose to share through forms or email.

How we use information

We use information to run the website, diagnose issues, improve content and performance, and respond to enquiries. Where practical, access is limited to people and providers who need it to support operations.

What we do not do

We do not intentionally sell visitor personal information. We also do not ask visitors to send regulated, sensitive, or confidential material through public forms unless an appropriate agreement is already in place.

Analytics and third-party tools

Where relevant, this website may rely on hosting, analytics, content delivery, form, and email tools. These providers may process technical or usage data under their own policies. This page may be updated as the website tooling evolves.

Contact forms and email

If you contact Sheffy Adey through this website or email, we may keep the message thread and related details so we can respond, continue project discussions, and maintain service history.

Data retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary for website operation, communication, record-keeping, and legitimate business needs, unless a longer period is required by applicable law.

Your choices

You may choose not to submit forms or emails. You can also request an update or removal of contact records where practical and legally permissible.

International visitors

Visitors may access this website from multiple regions. Data may be processed in locations where our providers operate, subject to their safeguards and policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact admin@sheffyadey.com.

For general enquiries, contact hello@sheffyadey.com.
